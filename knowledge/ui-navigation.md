## Quest Interface

### Starting a Quest

1. Click **Questing Portal** on the main map
2. Choose quest type:
   - **Profession Quests** (Mining, Gardening, Fishing, Foraging)
   - **Training Quests** (STR, AGI, INT, etc.)
3. Select your hero(es)
   - Check stamina (must have enough)
   - Check profession level (if applicable)
4. Click **Start Quest**
5. Confirm transaction in wallet

### Active Quests

- View in **Quest Log** or **Professions** tab
- See time remaining (if any)
- Can complete quests early if status allows

### Completing Quests

1. Go back to **Questing Portal**
2. Click **Complete** on finished quests
3. Confirm transaction
4. Rewards auto-added to wallet

## Hero Management

### Viewing Your Heroes

1. Click **Tavern** or **Profile** ‚Üí **Heroes**
2. Browse your hero collection
3. Click individual hero for details:
   - Stats (STR, AGI, INT, etc.)
   - Level
   - Profession levels
   - Summon count
   - Rarity
   - Generation

### Sending Heroes on Quests

1. **Option A**: Via Questing Portal ‚Üí Select heroes
2. **Option B**: Click hero card ‚Üí **Send on Quest**

### Leveling Heroes

- Heroes gain XP from quests automatically
- Level-ups happen when XP threshold is reached
- Higher level = better efficiency in professions

### Summoning New Heroes

1. Go to **Tavern**
2. Click **Summon**
3. Select 2 heroes to summon (one must be yours)
   - You can rent another hero from marketplace
4. Pay summon cost (JEWEL/CRYSTAL + fees)
5. Confirm transaction
6. Receive new hero (stats determined by parents + RNG)

## Garden Interface

### Adding Liquidity

1. Click **Gardens** or **Druid**
2. Select **Add Liquidity**
3. Choose token pair (e.g., CRYSTAL-USDC)
4. Enter amount for one token (other auto-calculates)
5. Approve tokens (first time only)
6. Confirm **Add Liquidity** transaction
7. Receive LP tokens

### Staking LP Tokens

1. After adding liquidity, click **Stake**
2. Select the garden/pool
3. Enter amount of LP tokens to stake
4. Confirm transaction
5. Start earning rewards

### Assigning Heroes to Gardens

1. Go to **Professions** ‚Üí **Gardening**
2. Select the garden you've staked in
3. Click **Assign Heroes**
4. Choose heroes with high INT/WIS
5. Confirm transaction
6. Heroes boost your yield

### Checking Rewards

1. Go to your staked garden
2. View **Pending Rewards**
3. Click **Claim** to harvest
4. Rewards sent to wallet

### Withdrawing from Gardens

1. Go to **Gardens**
2. Click **Unstake** on your LP position
3. Wait for cooldown (if any)
4. Click **Remove Liquidity**
5. Confirm transaction
6. Receive both tokens back

## Token Swapping (Trader)

1. Click **Trader** on main map
2. Select **From** token
3. Select **To** token
4. Enter amount
5. Check exchange rate and fees
6. Click **Swap**
7. Confirm in wallet

## Wallet Connection

### Connecting Wallet

1. Click **Connect Wallet** (top right)
2. Choose wallet provider:
   - MetaMask
   - WalletConnect
   - Others depending on chain
3. Approve connection in wallet popup
4. You're connected when you see your address

### Switching Networks

- Make sure wallet is on correct chain:
  - **Harmony** for Serendale (JEWEL)
  - **DFK Chain** for Crystalvale (CRYSTAL)
  - **Klaytn** for Serendale 2.0
- Switch in your wallet if needed

## Tips for New Players

### First Steps
1. Connect wallet
2. Acquire small amount of JEWEL/CRYSTAL
3. Buy 1-2 cheap heroes from marketplace
4. Send heroes on **training quests** to learn mechanics
5. Try a **foraging** or **fishing** quest (low stamina cost)

### Common Beginner Mistakes
- Not having enough gas token (ONE, JEWEL, KLAY) for transactions
- Sending hero on quest without checking stamina
- Forgetting to claim quest rewards
- Not understanding impermanent loss before adding liquidity

### Pro Tips
- **Bookmark the game** - URLs can change
- **Join Discord** for real-time updates
- **Check announcements** for events and changes
- **Use gardens for passive income** while learning other mechanics
- **Don't rush** - stamina regenerates, take your time to learn
