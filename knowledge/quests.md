# DeFi Kingdoms - Quests Knowledge Base

## Quest Types

### Profession Quests

#### Mining Quest
- **Goal**: Extract JEWEL/CRYSTAL tokens
- **Best Heroes**: Warriors, Knights, high STR/VIT
- **Stamina**: 5-25 per attempt
- **Rewards**: Locked JEWEL/CRYSTAL, XP, small chance for items
- **Strategy**: Use high-level heroes with Mining profession unlocked
- **Tip**: Mining is the primary way to earn native tokens

#### Gardening Quest
- **Goal**: Tend gardens, earn LP rewards
- **Best Heroes**: Wizards, Priests, high INT/WIS
- **Stamina**: 5-10 per session
- **Rewards**: LP tokens, compounding yields, XP
- **Strategy**: Deploy high-INT heroes to maximize rewards
- **Tip**: Lowest stamina cost, great for passive income

#### Fishing Quest
- **Goal**: Catch fish for pet care
- **Best Heroes**: Pirates, Thieves, high AGI/LCK
- **Stamina**: 5-15 per attempt
- **Rewards**: Fish, rare items, XP
- **Strategy**: Use high-LCK heroes for rare catches
- **Tip**: Essential for pet owners

#### Foraging Quest
- **Goal**: Gather plants and materials
- **Best Heroes**: Archers, Thieves, high AGI/DEX
- **Stamina**: 5-15 per attempt
- **Rewards**: Plants, crafting materials, XP
- **Strategy**: Speed-based, use agile heroes
- **Tip**: Materials used for future crafting systems

### Training Quests

#### Strength Training
- **Goal**: Increase STR stat
- **Stamina**: 5 per session
- **Rewards**: +1 STR (chance-based), XP
- **Best For**: Warriors, Knights, melee heroes

#### Agility Training
- **Goal**: Increase AGI stat
- **Stamina**: 5 per session
- **Rewards**: +1 AGI, XP
- **Best For**: Thieves, Archers, Ninjas

#### Intelligence Training
- **Goal**: Increase INT stat
- **Stamina**: 5 per session
- **Rewards**: +1 INT, XP
- **Best For**: Wizards, Sages

#### Wisdom Training
- **Goal**: Increase WIS stat
- **Stamina**: 5 per session
- **Rewards**: +1 WIS, XP
- **Best For**: Priests, Summoners

#### Endurance Training
- **Goal**: Increase END stat
- **Stamina**: 5 per session
- **Rewards**: +1 END, XP
- **Best For**: Knights, DarkKnights

#### Dexterity Training
- **Goal**: Increase DEX stat
- **Stamina**: 5 per session
- **Rewards**: +1 DEX, XP
- **Best For**: Archers, Ninjas

#### Vitality Training
- **Goal**: Increase VIT stat
- **Stamina**: 5 per session
- **Rewards**: +1 VIT, XP
- **Best For**: Warriors, Paladins

#### Luck Training
- **Goal**: Increase LCK stat
- **Stamina**: 5 per session
- **Rewards**: +1 LCK, XP
- **Best For**: Pirates, Summoners

## Quest Strategies by Goal

### Goal: Maximum XP
**Recommended Quests**:
1. Training quests (low stamina, guaranteed XP)
2. Profession quests at appropriate level
3. Any quest your hero is specialized for

**Strategy**: Run multiple heroes on training simultaneously

### Goal: Maximum Gold (JEWEL/CRYSTAL)
**Recommended Quests**:
1. Mining (primary token source)
2. Gardening (compound LP yields, sell rewards)
3. Fishing for sellable rare items

**Strategy**: Focus high-STR heroes on mining, high-INT on gardens

### Goal: Materials/Items
**Recommended Quests**:
1. Foraging for plants
2. Fishing for fish and rare drops
3. Mining for occasional item drops

**Strategy**: Use high-LCK heroes to boost rare drop rates

### Goal: Profession Leveling
**Recommended Quests**:
1. Repeatedly run the profession quest you want to level
2. Use heroes with matching stat bonuses
3. Level hero overall to unlock profession bonuses

**Strategy**: Specialize heroes early in one profession

## Stamina Management

- **Stamina regenerates over time**: Full refill takes 24-48 hours depending on hero
- **Quest stamina costs vary**: 5-25 per quest
- **Strategy**: Rotate heroes to always have someone ready
- **Tip**: Higher-level heroes use stamina more efficiently (better yield per stamina)

## Quest Cooldowns

- Most quests have no cooldown
- Heroes can quest as long as they have stamina
- Some special quests may have timers (check specific realm)

## Rewards

- **XP**: All quests grant experience
- **Gold**: Mining, selling quest rewards
- **Items**: Profession quests, rare drops from high-LCK heroes
- **Stats**: Training quests (chance-based increases)
